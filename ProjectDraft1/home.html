Endangered Species

Many animals around the world are endangered due to climate change and human activities such as poaching, hunting and illegal trading.

Highlighted below are the places where some endangered species live. Go to the menu to learn more about each animal.

Worldwide, there are over 41,000 endangered species, and the number is growing due to human actions against the environment. Climate Change, Deforestation, and Poaching are all common reasons for an animal becoming endangered.

Below shows a map of where 3 endangered species, the Ringed Seal, the Asian Elephant, and the Amur Leopard, currently live.
